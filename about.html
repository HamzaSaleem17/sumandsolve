About Sum & Solve

Sum & Solve is a free, adaptive math quiz platform designed to help learners of all ages improve their addition skills in a fun and engaging way. We believe that practice should be rewarding, with clear progression and instant feedback.

Our mission is simple: make math practice accessible, interactive, and motivating — whether you're a student, teacher, or just love brain training.

How It Works

  • Adaptive difficulty across multiple levels.
  • Scoring system: +5 for correct, −2 for incorrect.
  • Level up after 20 correct answers or 20 attempts per level.
